List of the Greatest Basketball Players Ever

My Personal Top 10 Picks

Honorable Mentions to the Legends

Debating the greatest basketball players of all time is one of the hardest discussions in sports. This list evaluates players based on their career achievements, championship rings, MVP awards, and overall dominance during their career.

While opinions vary across different generations of fans, these ten athletes have left an undeniable mark on the history of the game.

Ranking these players is tough because the game has changed so much. However, anyone on this list is a real legend of the game who played with tons ofpassion and heart.

Here is my official top 10:

  1. Michael Jordan
  2. LeBron James
  3. Kobe Bryant
  4. Wilt Chamberlain
  5. Larry Bird
  6. Steph Curry
  7. Shaquille Oneal
  8. Bill Russell
  9. Kareem Abdul Jabar
  10. Magic Johnson
